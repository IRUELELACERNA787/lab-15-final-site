Year 88 of the Red Age

he Last Message from the Bastion Brain

It has been nearly 9 decades of war and fighting, of death and devastation throughout the world. I am known as The Bastion Brain, leader of this city and overseer of the Fixers. Normally, I would not reach out to a band of newly recruited Fixers like yourselves, but my cabinet tells me that it's good for morale if people know I do truly exist, and that I am still alive. You all have signed up for an arduous task, and I would not dare to say that you’ll all do fine. The monstrosities we call the Aberrants, the people who seek to turn this city into a cesspool of filth and grime, the losses you’re sure to come into contact with during your job; these are all things that place immense strain on your physical and mental health. Just know, as Fixers, you are free to resign at any time. Nobody will judge you. But, we are in desperate need of your abilities. Anybody who’s been inducted as a Fixer has talent, and so if you’re here, you’ll certainly be able to handle at least a few months of this work. I just hope that you all survive long enough to reap the benefits of this trade. With this, I wish you all the best of luck in your endeavors. May you find your way through this unrelenting hell.
